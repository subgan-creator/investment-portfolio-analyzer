What is Look-Through Analysis?

The Problem: Target date funds (like "Target Date 2045 Fund") appear as single holdings in your 401(k) statements, but they actually contain multiple underlying investments across different asset classes (stocks, bonds, international, etc.).

The Solution: By uploading your fund profile document, the analyzer can "look through" the target date fund to see its true composition. This gives you accurate:

  • Diversification scores that reflect actual asset allocation
  • Sector exposure including the underlying holdings
  • True equity vs. fixed income split
  • Better concentration risk assessment
